How to make coffee in a chemex

Table of Contents

I. What you'll need

  • Grounded coffee
  • Chemex glassware
  • Hot water
  • Coffee filter (one of the two)
    • Paper filter
    • Cloth filter

II. Steps

Pour a little bit of hot water into the chemex to warm it up, then discard the water into the sink. Place the filter of your choice on top of the glassware, add your desired amount of grounded coffee. Then slowly begin to pour hot water in gentle circles. Allow it to brew. Coffee will gradually gather at the bottom of the chemex. Repeat until you reach your desired amount of liquid coffee. Rinse the filter if using cloth, throw out if using paper. Pour the coffee into a cup.
